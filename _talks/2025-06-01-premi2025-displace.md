Presented our paper *Adaptive Multi-Scale Speaker Diarization on DISPLACE 2024 Challenge Task* at the 11th International Conference on Pattern Recognition and Machine Intelligence (PReMI 2025). The talk covered our adaptive multi-scale speaker diarization approach for the DISPLACE 2024 Challenge, which targets far-field, multilingual conversational speech. The work is co-authored with Ravindrakumar M. Purohit and Hemant Patil, and published in Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science.
