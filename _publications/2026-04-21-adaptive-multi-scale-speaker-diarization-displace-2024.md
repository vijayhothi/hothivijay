This paper proposes an adaptive multi-scale speaker diarization framework for difficult audio conditions, with a particular emphasis on overlap handling and robust speaker segmentation. The approach was evaluated on the DISPLACE 2024 Challenge benchmark dataset.

**Key Contributions**
- Proposed an adaptive multi-scale diarization framework
- Improved speaker segmentation in challenging audio conditions
- Applied deep neural speaker embedding techniques
- Enhanced diarization performance for overlapping speech
- Utilized multi-scale temporal feature analysis
